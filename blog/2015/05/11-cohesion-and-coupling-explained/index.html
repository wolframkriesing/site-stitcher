Cohesion and Coupling explained

Wolfram Kriesing - 2015-05-11 13:00 CET - tagged with: #oop #coupling #cohesion #classes

Wikipedia says about cohesion, it refers to the degree to which the elements of a module belong together. And about coupling it says how closely connected two routines or modules are.
