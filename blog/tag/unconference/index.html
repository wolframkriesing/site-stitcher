JSCamp Barcelona - Recap [this is a draft]

July 21, 2018 - tagged with: #events #unconference #open space

It is over, the JSCamp in Barcelona, formerly named Angular Camp. These had been two intense days of JavaScript, organized by David Pich. This year about 500 people came together to spend two days in a single track conference, listening to topics like v8, webpack, nodejs, npm, Rust, ionic, nextjs, a lot of vuejs and much more.
